# AI PM Skills Taxonomy

11 skills across 4 categories for mapping Maven course syllabi.

---

# A. AI Product Development

## A1. Strategy & Feasibility
Identifying AI opportunities, judging what's realistic, roadmapping, and prioritization.

## A2. PRD & Specs
Writing AI product specs, defining guardrails, acceptable failure rates.

## A3. Eval & Iteration
Measuring AI quality, building eval datasets, prompt iteration, A/B testing.

## A4. Shipping & Lifecycle
Prototype to production, monitoring, reliability, managing the full product lifecycle.

## A5. Prototyping & Vibe Coding
Using AI coding tools to rapidly build functional prototypes and ship products without traditional engineering.

---

# B. AI Technical Skills

## B1. Models & Selection
Understanding model capabilities/limits, comparing models, cost/latency/quality tradeoffs.

## B2. System Architecture
Embeddings, vector databases, APIs, inference infrastructure.

## B3. Agents & RAG
Building agents, RAG systems, multi-step workflows.

---

# C. PM Leadership

## C1. Communication & Influence
Storytelling, presenting to execs, persuading without authority, stakeholder management, managing up.

## C2. Management & Team Dev
Feedback, delegation, hiring, 1:1s, new manager fundamentals, executive presence.

---

# D. Design

## D1. Design
Design systems, visual design, behavioral design, AI-native UX patterns.
